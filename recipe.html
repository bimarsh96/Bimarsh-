Tasty Momo Recipe

Ingredients

Here are the essential ingredients for making delicisous momo

  • Dough:2 Cups All purpose Flour
  • Water as needed
  • 1/2 Teaspoon salts
  • Fillings:500g Chicken
  • 1 Small Onion,finely chopped
  • Garlic cloves,Minced
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger paste
  • 2 Green chilly ,finely chopped
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 1 Tablespoon soy sauce
  • Timur per taste

Directions

  1. Prepare the dough ,In a box,mix the floor with salt ,gradually add water and kneed until you form a smooth dough.Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the fillings,In another bowl combine the chicken,chopped onion,garlic,ginger,reen chillies and mix well, Season with salt,pepper and soy sauce according to taste.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls,Roll each ball into a thin circular disc.
  4. Place a spoonful of fillings in the center of each disc
  5. Fold the edges and press to seal properly,
  6. Steam the momo in streamer for around 15-20 minutes.
